I am a doctoral student at Ben-Gurion University of the Negev, focusing on database systems.
My current research interests include data provenance, responsible data management, and query optimization methods.

As an undergrad, I was affiliated with DBGroup at the Illinois Institute of Technology (now at UIC). Check them out!
